Bio

This summer, I am interning at Matroid where I'll be primarily developing Computer Vision solutions for Matroid's platform.

I am currently pursuing my PhD in Computer Science at University of Maryland, Baltimore County advised by Dr. Hamed Pirsiavash. My area of research is Computer Vision. I have spent a significant amount of time working on adversarial attacks and defenses - especially for object detection. I have also worked on the currently developing area of poisoning/backdoor attacks on Convolutional Neural Networks.

Prior to this, I was a Software Engineer at Samsung Research Institute Bangalore, India where I was part of the DRAM Group in the Memory Team of Samsung Semiconductor India Research.

I spend my time taking photos, blogging, playing football, watching good movies and listening to music. I am an avid follower of sports and a supporter of Manchester United FC.
